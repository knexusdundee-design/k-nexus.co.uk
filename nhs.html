📅 Appointments

Book GP, specialist, and social prescribing appointments directly through the K-NEXUS hub. No phone queues. Staff will assist anyone who needs help with the process.

  • Same-day urgent appointment support
  • Translation & advocacy service available
  • Dedicated veterans appointment pathway

💊 Prescriptions

Collect, order, and track NHS prescriptions through the hub. We partner with local pharmacies to ensure no-one misses medication due to access barriers.

  • Prescription collection point on-site
  • Repeat prescription reminder service
  • Emergency medication signposting

🧠 Mental Health

NHS Tayside Social Prescribing pathway. Access counselling, crisis support, and peer mental health services — all under one roof with no waiting room stigma.

  • 1-to-1 counselling (referral via hub)
  • Crisis drop-in — no appointment needed
  • Peer support groups: veterans, young people, neurodivergent
  • Art, music & movement therapy

♿ Accessibility & Inclusion

All NHS services at K-NEXUS are designed from the ground up for inclusion. Every room is accessible. Every person is met where they are.
