🎬 Creation Labs

K-NEXUS's professional creative production suite — recording booths, animation stations, music production, anime dubbing, film editing, and a full podcast setup. Open to all members. No experience needed to start. Qualifications available for those who want to go further.

🎙 Recording Booths

  • 3 soundproofed vocal booths
  • Podcast recording — full setup, live-ready
  • Anime dub sessions — script, record, sync
  • Voice-over work — member booking
  • ElevenLabs AI voice tools (via Rift Voice Studio)

🎵 Music Production

  • DAW stations — Logic, Ableton, FL Studio
  • MIDI keyboards, drum pads, guitar inputs
  • Mixing & mastering sessions (supervised)
  • K-NEXUS Rap branch — dedicated hip-hop bay
  • Release to Spotify/SoundCloud via hub pipeline

🎨 Animation & Film

  • Animation tablets — Wacom, iPad Pro
  • Adobe Creative Suite, DaVinci Resolve
  • Stop-motion rig
  • Green screen bay (see Prop Room)
  • Storyboard walls — physical + digital

🎓 Qualifications

SQA-accredited pathway available through K-NEXUS for creative media, music production, and digital arts. Scouts badge programme maps to creation skills (ages 9–99).
