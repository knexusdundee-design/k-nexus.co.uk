The K-NEXUS Ecosystem
Four pillars. One roof. Built for Dundee.
🏥
NEXUS CARE
NHS Social Prescribing integration. GP referral pathway direct into K-NEXUS wellbeing services. No waiting lists. Walk-in and referred.
🌊
NEXUS WAVE
Events and live experiences brand. Community shows, open mic nights, expos, conventions. K-NEXUS Live is coming.
📡
NEXUS PULSE
Safety and RFID infrastructure. Member access control, real-time tracking, missing-person alert protocol. Every door. Every card. Always watching.
🔒
NEXUS VAULT
Stock management, inventory, lending library, vending, and asset tracking. Every item accounted for. Powered by Rift AI.
NEXUS SCOUTS — AGES 9 TO 99
Activity-based community badge programme. No upper age limit. No judgment. Covering creativity, digital skills, community service, sport, and wellbeing. Badges potentially converting to SQA qualifications.
