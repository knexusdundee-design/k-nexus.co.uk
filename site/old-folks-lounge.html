Old Folks Lounge

K-NEXUS Community Sanctuary Hub — Dundee

🍵 Old Folks Lounge

A warm, quiet, genuinely welcoming space for elderly community members. Not a patronising "senior room" — a proper lounge. Comfortable chairs, tea always on, calm acoustics, familiar faces. The kind of place your nan would actually want to spend a Tuesday afternoon.

☕ Always On

  • Free tea & coffee — always. No exceptions
  • Hot meals from the Café soup kitchen (delivered in)
  • Biscuits. Obviously
  • Morning paper & tablet stations for those who want them

🤝 Community & Company

  • Volunteer companionship programme — trained K-NEXUS volunteers
  • Board games, cards, dominoes — always available
  • Gentle music (jazz, folk, classical) — mute toggle at desk
  • TV corner — news, nature, sport, no pressure

🏥 NHS & Support Links

  • Social prescribing drop-in — Tuesday & Thursday
  • Medication reminder support
  • GP appointment booking assistance
  • Falls prevention advice (NHS Tayside partnership)

🖥 Digital Skills

Friendly, unjudgmental help with phones, tablets, video calls, and online banking. No jargon. No rushing. Run by trained K-NEXUS volunteers and Nexus 4 branch.
