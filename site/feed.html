📱 K-NEXUS Social Feed

A community social platform owned by the community. No algorithm deciding what you see. No shadow banning. No ads. Chronological timeline — your follows first, then everything else by time. What you post reaches everyone who follows you. Guaranteed.

📤 Post Anything

  • Video — up to 10 minutes (free), longer for Explorer+
  • Photos, galleries, illustrations
  • Text posts, threads
  • Audio clips — music, podcasts, voice notes
  • Direct from Prop Room or Creation Labs

📡 Go Live

  • 500 followers threshold — half of TikTok's requirement
  • Multi-camera support (from Drama Department stage)
  • Live from any hub room — dedicated Wi-Fi infrastructure
  • Recording auto-saved to your profile

Don't start from zero. Import your TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube content and following via official Data Portability APIs — legal, GDPR-safe, no scraping. Connect once, bring your audience.

  • Instagram data export + OAuth
  • YouTube channel import

  • No viral boosting for paying brands
