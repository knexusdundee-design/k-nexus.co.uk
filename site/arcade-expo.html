🕹 The Arcade Expo

A proper arcade — not a pub side-room with two broken machines. Retro cabinets, modern rigs, pinball, light-gun games, rhythm machines, crane games, and a competitive esports bay. All under neon signs and ambient synth. The kind of place you stay for five hours without noticing.

🎮 Retro Cabinet Hall

  • Classic arcade cabinets — Street Fighter, Pac-Man, Galaga, Tekken, DDR
  • Pinball machines — rotating selection
  • Light-gun shooters
  • Crane & prize machines
  • Token or contactless pay — member card discount

⚡ Esports Bay

  • High-spec gaming PCs — 144hz, RTX, peripherals
  • Console stations — PS5, Xbox Series X, Switch
  • Ranked nights — weekly tournaments
  • Coaching sessions for young people
  • Stream-ready rigs — go live from your seat

🎵 Rhythm & Immersive

  • Dance Dance Revolution
  • Taiko no Tatsujin (Japanese drum)
  • Beat Saber VR booth (linked to VR room)
  • Ambient synthwave soundtrack — mute toggle at bay

🏆 Tournament Board

Live leaderboards on K-Wall display. Monthly championship events — prizes from the K-NEXUS Shop. Open sign-up via member portal. Explorer+ members get priority seeding.
