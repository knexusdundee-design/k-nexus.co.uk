Vending Factory

K-NEXUS Community Sanctuary Hub — Dundee

🗾 Japanese Vending Zone

Authentic Japanese convenience culture brought to Dundee. Hot ramen, ice cups, microwave stations, onigiri, and 24-hour snack access.

  • Hot ramen vending — 4 flavours
  • Ice cup machine (slushie / crushed)
  • Hot water station (instant noodles)
  • Microwave bay (1-min cook meals)
  • Onigiri & Japanese snack packs
  • Matcha / green tea vending
  • Manga reading area adjacent
  • 24-hour access for members

🇰🇷 Korean Street Food Zone

K-pop culture meets K-NEXUS. Korean noodles, hot pot kits, tteokbokki, and 24-hour snack station — curated for community, priced for real people.

  • Korean instant noodles (5 varieties)
  • Hot pot kits — self-cook bay
  • Tteokbokki & street snacks
  • Korean iced drinks machine
  • 24h snack station (member card)
  • K-pop playlist ambient audio

📺 K-Wall Digital Display

The K-Wall sits adjacent to the vending zone — a live digital display showing products, prices, QR codes, community announcements, and live stock levels.
