🎭 The Prop Room

Book a slot. Grab your costume. Shoot your video. Edit it here. Post it from here. The Prop Room is K-NEXUS's full content creation stage — costumes, lighting rigs, green-screen, props, and a built-in editing suite. Linked directly to the social feed for instant publish.

🗄 Inventory — What's In Here

  • 200+ costumes — anime, fantasy, sci-fi, historical, seasonal
  • Props: weapons (safe/foam), crowns, tech, everyday
  • Green-screen — full height, tracked lighting
  • Ring lights, LED panels, coloured gels
  • Reflectors, diffusers, wireless lapel mics
  • Teleprompter rig for talking-head content

📅 Booking

Book via the member portal — 30, 60, or 90-minute slots. Costumes reserved when you book. Group bookings welcome (max 6 per session).

  • Free for Explorer+ members (2 sessions/month)
  • Pay-per-use: £5 / 30min
  • School & community group rates — contact hub

✂ In-App Editor

Shoot your video, then edit on-site — trim, dub voices (ElevenLabs), add soundtracks from the K-NEXUS library, overlay storyboard panels, export at full quality.

  • Trim & cut
  • Voice dub (AI or record live)
  • Royalty-free soundtrack library
  • Export to Feed, TikTok, YouTube, or local download

📱 Direct to Feed

Once edited, post directly to the K-NEXUS social feed — or export to TikTok / YouTube / Instagram via the connected accounts panel. No file transfers, no emails. One tap.
