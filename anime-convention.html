Anime Convention Hall

K-NEXUS Community Sanctuary Hub — Dundee

🎌 What Is It?

K-NEXUS's Anime Convention Hall is a permanent cosplay and fan culture space — not a one-weekend event, but a living, year-round home for anime, manga, gaming, and Japanese pop culture in Dundee. Think crowded convention energy baked into a space that's open every day.

🛍 Merch & Stalls

Rotating vendor stalls — independent artists, fan merch, official imports, custom prints, figurines. Member artists get discounted pitch slots. New drops every month.

  • Independent artist showcase
  • Japanese import goods
  • Custom commissions board
  • Monthly themed pop-up stalls

🎤 Panels & Screenings

Community-run panels, watch parties, dub sessions, manga swaps. Big screen for simulcast premieres and event broadcasts. No gatekeeping — beginners and obsessives equally welcome.

  • Weekly screening nights
  • Guest creator panels (quarterly)
  • Cosplay workshops & tutorials
  • Manga library — borrow & read on-site

📸 Cosplay Zone

Dedicated photo area with themed backdrops, ring lighting, and props. Linked directly to the Prop Room — plan your look, build your costume, shoot it, post it.

Backdrops Ring lights Prop Room linked All levels welcome

🎮 Anime Gaming Corner

Dedicated consoles loaded with JRPG, fighting games, and visual novels. Tournaments run monthly. Linked to the Arcade Expo next door.

JRPG library Fighting game tournaments Visual novels

🧠 Neurodivergent-Friendly Design

Quiet hours operate daily 10am–12pm. Sensory-safe corner always available. Noise-cancelling zone adjacent to main hall. All events subtitled where possible.
