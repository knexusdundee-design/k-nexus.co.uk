📚 Dogear Dens

K-NEXUS's reading and quiet study spaces. Named for the best kind of books — loved enough to be worn. A proper library feel without the library rules. Comfy chairs, actual silence enforced, manga wall, physical book swap, and study booths for those who need to focus.

📖 The Collection

  • 3,000+ physical books — fiction, non-fiction, graphic novels
  • Full manga wall — 500+ volumes, borrow or read on-site
  • Community book swap shelf — bring one, take one
  • Large-print section
  • Audiobook tablets — headphones provided

🖊 Study Booths

  • 6 individual study booths — soundproofed, desk lamp, power
  • Bookable in 2-hour slots (free for members)
  • Wi-Fi — dedicated high-speed line
  • Whiteboard wall in group study room (seats 8)
  • Printing available — first 10 pages free/month

🎮 Quiet Gaming

A small selection of quiet, solo games for those who want to decompress without screens. Puzzles, chess, strategy card games. No noise. No commentary. Just thinking.

📅 Events

  • Book club — weekly, all genres welcome
  • Manga reading nights
  • Author visits (quarterly)
  • Children's story time — Saturday mornings
